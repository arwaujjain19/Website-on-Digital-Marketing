    Vision and Mission of Digital Dojo.

    Digital Dojo OU is focused on its vision and mission of digitally transforming the world.

    In this endeavor, it is primarily the human resources that need to be re-oriented and guided on acquiring the right digital skills. Driven by the right digital strategies and armed with digitally skilled resources, organizations can quickly embark on a digital journey to re-orient themselves and the world.

    Vision Statement - A resourceful Entrepreneurial World where people are not just excellent job performers but also amazing job creators, learning quickly the latest digital skills that impact industries and the global economy.

    Mission Statement - By 2025, empower the lives of 1 million+ people globally with various digital skills like Digital Marketing, IoT (Internet of Things), Automation, Machine Learning, Cognitive Computing and Block-chain technologies.

    Our Philosophy - Digital Dojo's philosophy is very simple. We want to help people and brands prosper in the digital economy. We want to positively transform our customers and prepare them for the digital economy. Transformation can happen only with the right digital skills and by implementing the right digital strategies. We help our customers in every step as they take giant strides into the digital world.
